NVBA is proud to host Durga Puja 2021 For Blissful Three Days Of Puja Celebration From October 8th To October 10th, 2021. Our Goal Is To Provide You With The Same Feeling As In Previous Years.. NVBA Always Plans Top-Class Entertainment Featuring The Best International And Local Artists In The Line-Up. NVBA RangaMancha Will Bring You Three Nights Of Cultural Entertainment with the legendary Kavita Krishnamurthy.

The puja is never the same without the sounds of “dhaak”, ulu dhwani, conches, the smell of incense and bhog. Last year has been challenging in many ways as most of our lives are disrupted by the global pandemic. This time the midst of this ongoing calamity, we bring happy tidings in persone Durga Puja.

Our goal is to provide you with the same feeling as past years. Maintaining the tradition and continuity we will be performing Durga Puja ! More details will be shared in the coming days.
