Saraswati Pujo

Saraswati puja is celebrated by Hindus all across the globe. This festival is primarily significant for scholars and students who worship Goddess Saraswati with dedication to acquire wisdom and knowledge.

NVBA is pleased to announce details for our most awaited Saraswati Pujo.

Date

Saturday, 1st February 2020

Location

Mercer Middle School, 42149 Greenstone Dr, Aldie, VA 20105

Timing

9am to 10pm

NVBA is pleased to announce the children's events for Saraswati Puja.

1) চলো ছবি আঁকি (Cholo Chhobi Aanki) (Visual Arts)

Participants in each group will be given 30 minutes to complete the artwork. Art paper, pencil, and crayons will be provided. No personal art supplies and stationery would be allowed.

Age Groups & Visual Arts Themes

5 Years - 7 Years: My Favorite Story
8 Years - 9 Years: My Favorite Trip
10 Years - 12 Years: My Dream

2) বাংলা কবিতা আবৃত্তি (Bangla Kobita Abritti) (Bangla Poem Recitation)

Participants will recite the poem assigned to their specific group. The poems are attached for ease of reference. Please find the links to these poems below for your reference.

Age Groups & Poems

5 Years - 7 Years: Sukumar Ray's Baburam Sapure (From Abol Tabol)
8 Years - 9 Years: Rabindranath Tagore's Taal Gaach (From Shishu)
10 Years - 12 Years: Kazi Nazrul Islam's Khuki O Kathbirali

All the participants, who pre-register for the event, will receive a participation certificate.
