Saraswati Puja - 2022

 

Please note the points below to get the best drive-through Pujo experience!
On February 5th 2022, Saturday.

When you arrive at the drive through:
  1. Follow volunteer instructions and lookout for directions to approach the Pujo location. Prasad/Raffle/Instructions will all be provided directly to your car at designated locations during the drive through.
  2. Do not get out of your car at any time during the drive-through. If you want to come out of car, please approach designated parking area, park your car and then come out with masks on at all times.
  3. Do not forget to collect and keep your raffle ticket safe, you don’t want to miss out on those exciting prizes, lots of prizes to be won ( winners will be announced at the end of live pujo telecast. More winners will be announced during the virtual cultural program on 12th)
  4. Tune in to FM 107.1 for the live pujo broadcast.
  5. Try to bring your own equipment for Hate Khari (slate & chalk available in Michael’s and Walmart). We will try to provide if you aren’t able to bring one ( subject to availability)
  6. Please visit our website (novaba.org) for live telecast of the Pujo and programs

Saraswati Pujo virtual Cultural event participation and Art competition!!

Cultural event's participation entries has been closed for this year.

Please read through and adhere to the following instructions before submission.
  • Children aged 5-18 are welcome to participate
  • Preference will be given to group participation to provide opportunities for maximum participation
  • Each participant can take part in only one program
  • As a humble reminder, families of participating children are required to be registered NVBA members. If you are a new member or failed to renew last year, please go to https://www.novaba.org/user and do the needful. Activated memberships remain valid for one year.

চলো ছবি আঁকি (Cholo Chhobi Aanki)

Children’s virtual art competition will be held via live Zoom 4-5PM on February 5th. Zoom link will be emailed to participants before February 5th. Results will be announced on February 12th during Saraswati Puja Cultural Program.

Please pre-register your child using this Cholo Chhobi Aanki - form by January 31st.

Participants in each age group will be given 45 minutes to complete the artwork virtually through Zoom. Participants will use their personal art supplies and will need to focus the webcam at their workspace during the Zoom session. Please use the art paper of standard size 9” x 12” or 8” x 10” or A4 8.3” x 11.7”.

The competition will be held in three age groups: Group 1: 5 - 8 Years Group 2: 9 - 12 Years Group3: 13 - 18 Years Art topics will be given on the spot.

Children’s virtual art competition will be held via live Zoom 4-5PM on February 5th.

Children can participate in both cultural program and art competition. For further questions and more information email cultural.novaba@yahoo.com.
