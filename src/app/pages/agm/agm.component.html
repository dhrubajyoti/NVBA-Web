Annual General Meeting (AGM)

Northern Virginia Bengali Association (NVBA) invites you to attend its Annual General Meeting (AGM)
on.

Date: December 5th, 2021 - Sunday
Venue: Chantilly Regional Library Meeting Room
Time: 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM.

Get Directions

The Election Committee would like to invite nominations for serving as members of the 2022-2023 NVBA Executive Committee (EC). Nominations may be submitted to the NVBA Election Committee via email to nvba_election_committee@yahoo.com by December 02, 2021. If any of the current EC members have not served two consecutive terms (4 years) and intend to serve in the new EC they should also send their nomination by email, as the existing EC will be dissolved on December 15, 2021.

As per the constitution, EC may have a minimum of seven and a maximum of eleven members. Nominations from the floor will only be opened if the Election Committee receives less than seven candidates. A voting process may be necessary if there are more than eleven candidates. Voting will only be open to currently registered NVBA members who are physically present during AGM.

The Agenda for the AGM is as follows:
  • State of NVBA (President) 3:00 PM - 3:30 PM
  • Review of un-audited Balance Sheet for the year 2021 (Treasurer). 3:30 PM - 4:00 PM
  • Election guidance, followed by the Election (Election Committee). 4:00 PM - 5 PM
  • Formation of new Executive committee (new EC members). 5 PM - 5:30 PM
  • Announcement of 3 Officer positions (President-Elect) 5:30 Pm - 6:00 PM
We encourage all nominees to familiarize themselves with the NVBA constitution.
We strongly urge all of you to attend this important meeting with your fellow NVBA friends.
