Durga Puja 2020

Designed to be enjoyed from the comfort of your home in your living/family room.

NVBA is proud to host Durga Puja 2020 For Blissful Three Days Of Virtual Puja Celebration From October 23rd To October 25th, 2020. Our Goal Is To Provide You With The Same Feeling As In Previous Years But In A Virtual Setup. NVBA Always Plans Top-Class Entertainment Featuring The Best International And Local Artists In The Line-Up. NVBA RangaMancha Will Bring You Three Nights Of Cultural Entertainment Through Its Online Streaming Platform.

The puja is never the same without the sounds of “dhaak”, ulu dhwani, conches, the smell of incense and bhog. The sight of Maa Durga’s glorious ensemble, dhunuchi naach, sandhya arti, sandhi puja, mingling with friends is integral part of our experience. This year has been challenging in many ways as most of our lives are disrupted by the global pandemic. In the midst of this ongoing calamity, we bring happy tidings.

Our goal is to provide you with the same feeling as previous years, but in a virtual setup. Maintaining the tradition and continuity we will be performing Durga Puja Online! More details will be shared in the coming days.

NVBA has planned a THREE DAYS Puja Extravaganza for you.

23rd October, 2020
Local Cultural Program and an evening with Aritra
24th October, 2020
Enjoy Virtual Durga Puja, Organized by NVBA. Then watch Aneweshaa Live in the evening.
25th October, 2020
Enjoy Virtual Durga Puja and Arati from Washington Kali Bari. In the evening enjoy the timeless hits of Usha Uthup and Jolly Mukherjee.
More details coming soon.

We are happy to announce that NVBA has sponsored Durga Puja, on October, 25th at Washington Kali Bari on behalf of the whole community.

Feast with food

What is a Bengali Festival without food? Food is a quintessential part of puja. NVBA brings some palatable mouth watering delicacies, to make your puja evenings special with friends, family and food.

  • Members ordering food should pick up their orders by showing their proof of purchase between 4 – 6 PM on October 24th and October 25th from their nearest pickup location which will be announced soon.
