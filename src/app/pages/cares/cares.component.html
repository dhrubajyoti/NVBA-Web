Support and fund charitable causes.
Serve the local community to address homelessness and food insecurity.

Join us, support us, donate your time, donate money.

Our Programs & Services

2020 Charities

Baking Kits for Firefighters

December 20th, 2020.

Every year in the past, we have provided home-made hot breakfast to firefighters in December to say our thanks for all the sacrifices they make year-round. This year, due to COVID that was not a viable option. Instead of home-cooked breakfast, we provided baking-kit gift bags so that the firefighters could bake their own cookies and cakes at home. A total of 220 firefighters from 8 local fire stations received these packages. This was sponsored, in part, by Dr. Dave Roy and his wife Dr. Theresa Huang.

Food Bags for Dulles South Pantry

December 14th, 2020.

With COVID’s impact on food security still a threat the team decided that we could do more this year to help with Food banks to address this issue. Cares, with the help of NVBA EC and EC families delivered 100 food bags (754 lbs) to DSPF taking our total food donation for the year to 4984 lbs. It was a rainy day of delivery, but that did not deter our volunteers in delivering the goods.

Thanksgiving Food Drive for Food For Others

November 11th and 25th, 2020.

This year’s annual Thanksgiving food drive for FFO was unlike any other year’s. With COVID restrictions firmly in place due to a raging second wave, none of our traditional avenues of collecting food were open to us. Our only option (except couple of boxes in Centerville Giants) was to launch an all out fundraiser to collect money to buy food for our annual donation to FFO. And that’s exactly what we did. A total of $3049 was raised and 4230 lbs of food was donated to FFO.

Fundraiser for Super Cyclone Amphan Relief Work

May 25th, 2020.

Super Cyclonic Storm Amphan was a powerful and catastrophic tropical cyclone that caused widespread damage in Eastern India, specifically West Bengal, in May 2020.

The supporters of NVBA wholeheartedly contributed $13,376. NVBA is closely working with organizations who are working on the ground (Ramakrishna Mission - Belur Math, Bharat Sevashram Sangha, Nimpith - Sri Ramakrishna Ashram, Mukti, Nish-Shabd Angeekar Welfare Association, several groups of selfless volunteers. NVBA worked with worthy organizations at the time of catastrophe.

Beddings for Katherine Hanley Family Shelter

April 21st, 2020.

As COVID was on the rise and funds were drying up for many homeless shelters we reached out to our partner Katherine Hanley Family Shelter to ask if any help was needed. They mentioned due to COVID related budget cuts they no longer had linen service for residents and desperately needed new beddings. The ask was for 50 new beddings for the residents. Our volunteers with the help of their friends and colleagues donated 50 requested beddings (valued $2000) through Amazon.

Pandemic fundraiser for DSP and other Charities

March 15th, 2020.

With the Pandemic news spreading and realities of what misery it could bring on the local population in need, we sprung to action to raise funds to help our local food bank partner, Dulles South Food pantry. Our goal was to raise a $1000 from NVBA general membership. We ended up raising $2000. We donated $1,200 to DSP and $500 to Kolkata Foundation for COVID help in Kolkata. We saved $300 for other charitable activities later in the year.

Park Clean-up @Cunnigham Park

March 1st, 2020.

At the request of Fairfax County Parks division our volunteers participated in removing invasive plants. 20 adults and 8 children participated in this fun event. We were looking forward to the followup event where we would go back for reseeding and planting native plants. Unfortunately, that never happened as the COVID pandemic news broke and most mass activities got canceled.

Fundraiser for Angawadi

February 1st, 2020.

2020 brought us a whole new initiative in the form of helping an Anganwadi school in Kalikapur stand on its feet. A day-care/school for 20 street children, the school was badly in need of supplies and structural help to make the place a better environment for the children. Our volunteers seized the opportunity of organizing misti/bake sale to raise funds during Saraswati Puja. $700 was raised through the sale and much needed help was provided to the Anganwadi school. Details at the end.

Kar-Seva at Raj Khalsa Gurudwara

January 3rd, 2020.

It was all about starting the year right with doing some volunteering kar-seva at Raj Khalsa Gurudwara in Sterling. This Gurudwara has been a great partner of ours over the years, so we never miss an opportunity to roll up our sleeves to help them in their kitchen. 2020 was no exception. Except, after January we could not go back for the rest of the year as things changed due to COVID.

Project Anganwadi-2020 Highlights

2020 Highlights

Funds raised for Anganwadi during Saraswati Puja and in the Fall were used for

  • Helping the school with structural improvements and class room improvements (plastic mats, plastic drums for keeping food supplies safe from rodents)
  • Providing weekly nutritional ration for the children during the COVID related school closure. As of 12/31…7 weeks of such nutrition has been provided. This is expected to go on till early part of 2021

Cares Extended Team Project for Sitarampur

Help for Sitarampur

Cares extended team, with the help of senior NVBA members like Samir Nandy and Tarun Roy and substantial financial assistance from Pulak Chakraborty and Ashok Motayed are feeding 100 children daily lunch in the red-light district of Sitarampur, near Asansol. The project started in August and is expected to continue into early 2021. Apart from daily food the children have been provided with basic instruction, new clothes for Durga Puja andnew sweaters for the winter months.
